Mrs. Blue Sky

Daniel Stigmon

Please Ms. Blue Sky

I need some aid

My fluffy white dog

Is starting to fade

It looks like a dragon

And now like a cow

It looks like the ring

Around the word “POW”

Please Ms. Blue Sky

Bring my friend back

Be it over that mountain

Or back in a sack

Bring him back on the wind

Like a frisbee in flight

Because playtime without him

just isn't alright.

I miss how we’d play

For an hour a day

How his fur would flop

In all sorts of ways

I’d throw a frisbee

It’d go right through

He was my best friend

I think… don’t you?

Oh! What’s that I see

At the edge of the sky

My dog doesn’t usually

go that high...

I suppose it is time

I best go and hide

My dogs turned to thunder

I’m going inside
